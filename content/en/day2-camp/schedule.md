## Session Details

### Opening (10:00 - 10:15)

**Content**:
- Host greetings
- Confirm today's goal
- Timetable explanation
- Notes (Wi-Fi, restrooms, etc.)

**Goal**:
> Create one "working thing" by the end of today and demo it for everyone

---

### Introductions & Idea Sharing (10:15 - 10:30)

**Format**: 1 minute per person

**What to share**:
1. Name, department
2. What you want to build today (in one sentence)
3. Your enthusiasm

**Points**:
- No need for a perfect explanation
- "I want to build something like this" is enough

---

### Development Time (10:30 - 12:00 / 1:00 PM - 4:30 PM)

**Format**: Individual development + mentor support

**How it works**:
- Develop on your own PC
- Ask neighbors or mentors when stuck
- "Can you take a look?" is very welcome
- Share progress briefly every 30 minutes or so (optional)

**Mentor role**:
- Walk around and support those having trouble
- Answer technical questions
- Give direction advice

---

### Lunch Networking (12:00 - 1:00 PM)

**Content**:
- Exchange ideas over lunch
- Share morning progress
- Discuss challenges and solutions
- Share questions and insights

**Points**:
- Taking a complete break is OK
- Continuing development is also OK

---

### Presentation Prep (4:30 PM - 5:00 PM)

**Tasks**:
- Verify demo works
- Organize presentation flow
- Take screenshots (insurance if demo doesn't work)

**What to present (3 minutes)**:
1. What you built (30 seconds)
2. Demo (1 minute 30 seconds)
3. What you worked on / struggled with (30 seconds)
4. What you want to do next (30 seconds)

---

### Show & Tell (5:00 PM - 5:45 PM)

**Format**: 3 minutes Ã— 8 people = about 25 minutes + Q&A

**Flow**:
1. Connect screen (HDMI or AirPlay)
2. 3 minutes for demo & explanation
3. Q&A time (1-2 questions)

**Notes**:
- It's OK if not complete
- Show "how far you got"
- Errors are fine, talk about the process

---

### Closing (5:45 PM - 6:00 PM)

**Content**:
- Overall reflection
- Share highlights
- Next camp announcement
- Survey request
- Group photo
