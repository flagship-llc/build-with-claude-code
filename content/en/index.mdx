# Build with Claude Code: Zero to One

*A Hands-On Workshop for Everyone*

Welcome to a workshop where you'll pair program with AI and build your own tool in just one day!

You can build something new from scratch, bring an existing project to develop further, or continue building after the workshop ends.

---

## About This Workshop

**No engineering experience required.** Even without programming experience, you can bring your ideas to life with Claude Code. You don't need to be an engineer in the traditional sense. All you need is the desire to create something useful. That's all it takes to make engineering happen.

**On-site support throughout.** Small group setting with mentors always available. Ask questions anytime and resolve doubts as you go. You won't be struggling alone.

**Expand your future options.** AI pair programming is a new skill for the modern era. By learning it now, you'll gain the ability to build your own tools for work or personal projects.

---

## What You Can Build

While **web apps** are the primary focus, you can also build **native apps** and other tools.

| Category | Examples |
|----------|----------|
| Web Apps | Dashboards, business management tools, form input tools, data visualization |
| Websites | Portfolio, landing pages, blogs |
| Smartphone Apps | iOS apps, Android apps |
| Desktop Apps | macOS apps, Windows apps |
| Automation & Bots | Slack bots, Chrome extensions, scheduled scripts |

### Shopify Apps

As a type of web app, Shopify app development for e-commerce operators is also possible.

- Inventory and order management extensions
- Customer analytics dashboards
- Custom feature additions

You can even publish to the [Shopify App Store](https://apps.shopify.com/). Additionally, Shopify's AI assistant "Sidekick" within the admin interface can also create apps.

Build whatever tool would make your work or life easier!

## Event Overview

- **Format**: 1-day workshop (10:00 AM - 6:00 PM)
- **Capacity**: ~8 participants (small group)
- **Target Audience**: No engineering experience required
- **Preparation**: Complete environment setup during Day 1 (Setup Session) before the main event. Day 1.5 (Pre-Development Period) tutorials are optional

### Participant Checklist

- [ ] Laptop (Mac)
- [ ] Charger
- [ ] Environment setup complete (from Setup Session)

### Support from Organizers

- **Mentor Support**: Mentors will assist you from environment setup through development
- **Claude Pro Plan**: Provided to all participants
- **Max Plan**: Upgrade available if token limits are reached
